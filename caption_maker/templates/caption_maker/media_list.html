Summary

I wanted to practice my Chinese by listening to music and then follow along on a script in order to memorize phrases, sentence structures, characters, etc. Unfortunately, I hadn't found a good way to do this aside from blankly staring at a word document or a piece of paper while playing the song off of YouTube. After failing to find chinese songs on YouTube with closed-captioning, I wondered if I could do it myself. So I made this and now I can, and so can you! I still have some work to do in order to make the user interface more friendly, but so far this is looking pretty good if I do say so myself.
